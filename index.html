A little bit about me.

I was born and raised in the beach cities of Greater Los Angeles. I've always loved technology, and I've always loved creating things. Eventually I came to find that I could explore both through web development.

I self-studied for a few years, then decided to take the plunge and enroll full-time at General Assembly. It was there that I really became fluent and learned the ins and outs of developing full-stack web applications.
